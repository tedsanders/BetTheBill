Bet the Bill is an easy way to split the bill at a restaurant. Here’s how it works:

Rather than forcing you to bother your server or dig out change or perform mental math, Bet the Bill randomly picks one member of your party to pay the entire bill. Your odds of being chosen are proportional to the cost of your meal, so no matter whether you got the Caesar salad or the sirloin steak, it's 100% fair.

Just type in the cost of each person’s meal and then tap ‘Bet the Bill.’ It’s that easy!
