Bet the Bill is the easiest way to split your restaurant bill and it's 100% fair.

To use Bet The Bill, just type in the cost of each person’s meal and then tap ‘Bet the Bill.’ It’s that easy! The app will choose someone to pay the entire bill

the hassles of bothering your server, digging out change, or trying to coordinate payments in Venmo/PayPal/Snapcash/Whatever.

Here’s how it works:

Instead of the typical solutions like bothering your server or digging out change, Bet the Bill randomly picks one member of your party to pay the entire bill. Your odds of paying are proportional to the cost of your meal, so whether you got the Caesar salad for six dollars or the sirloin steak for seventeen, it's 100% fair.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Is Bet the Bill really fair?

Yes. Bet the Bill is a locally run, open-source application with source code hosted at GitHub. Take a look at the source code and you'll see that fairness is guaranteed within the limits of floating point precision and whatever random number generator your javascript engine uses.

Will the luck balance out in the long run?

No. Randomness has no memory of your past bets, so the more often you bet, the more you stand to lose.

How do we handle tax and tip?

You don't even need to! As long as you agree that people should pay tax and tip proportional to the cost of their meal, then adding tax and tip is unnecessary, because it won't change the odds.

Can I use this for debts besides restaurant bills?

Yep!
